The Salon and Your Stylist

The salon is located in the original part of a 19th century house and was most recently used as an office in Daryl's personal residence. Recently, the salon has undergone renovations to retrofit the room into a full hair salon set-up.

Come right on in at your appointment time. You'll be treated to a sanitary, well-organized and classic space for extra comfort and relaxation.

Office Hair Salon stylist Daryl Weeks is a licensed hair stylist with many years of experience. Known by her clients as a casual, upbeat and professional person, you'll quickly find communication easy and your time at the salon, laid-back.

Daryl offers a catered hair service where you, and only you, receive her attention at one time. Your time is valuable and therefore, you'll never be double booked.
